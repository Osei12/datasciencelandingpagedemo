What is Data Science?

Data science is an interdisciplinary field that uses scientific methods, processes, algorithms and systems to extract knowledge and insights from noisy, structured and unstructured data .Many statisticians, including Nate Silver, have argued that data science is not a new field, but rather another name for statistics . Others argue data science focuses on problems and techniques unique to digital data .The term “data science” has been traced back to 1974, when Peter Naur proposed it as an alternative name for computer science .In 1985, C.F.Jeff Wu used the term Data Science for the first time in a lecture given to the Chinese Academy of Sciences in Beijing .There is still no consensus on the definition of data science and it is considered by some to be a buzzword . The professional title of “data scientist” has been attributed to DJ Patil and Jeff Hammerbacher in 2008 .
